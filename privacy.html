AHBA FieldOps — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Operated by AHBA Development

AHBA FieldOps ("the app") is an internal field-operations tool used by authorized AHBA Development teams (technicians and sales agents) to receive dispatched job orders, record job status, upload proof-of-installation photos, and coordinate with the dispatcher. This policy explains what the app collects and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your assigned username and display name (used to sign in and identify your team).
  • Location — while you are signed in and on shift, the app records your device GPS location periodically and when you update a job status, so the dispatcher can coordinate field work and verify deployment. Location is collected only while you are logged in.
  • Photos — proof-of-installation and document photos that you choose to capture or upload for a job order.
  • Work data — job-order details, status updates, expenses, remittance/collection entries, attendance (time-in/out), and messages you send within the app.

How we use it

Information is used solely to operate the field-service workflow: dispatching and tracking job orders, verifying completed work, computing operational costs, coordinating teams, and internal record-keeping. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Sharing

Data is accessible only to authorized AHBA Development operations staff (dispatchers/administrators) through the operations console. Data is stored on our managed backend (Supabase). We do not share data with third parties except as required to provide the service or by law.

Data retention

Work records are retained for operational and audit purposes. Proof photos may be cleared from cloud storage after archiving. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting AHBA Development.

Permissions

The app requests Location (for field coordination) and Camera/Photos (to upload job proof). You can manage these permissions in your device settings; some features will not work if disabled.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact AHBA Development at admin@ahbafield.app.
