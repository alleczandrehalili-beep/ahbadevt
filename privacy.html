Privacy Policy

AHBA FieldOps · by AHBA Development

Effective date: 6 July 2026 · Last updated: 6 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AHBA Development ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the AHBA FieldOps mobile application and its related web console (together, the "Service").

AHBA FieldOps is a workforce / field-operations tool intended for use by authorized personnel — field technicians, sales agents, security personnel, dispatchers, and administrators of AHBA Development and its authorized subcontractors. It is not intended for the general public.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate field operations, dispatch work, verify completed jobs, and manage attendance.

Subscriber/customer details entered as part of a job order are business records processed on behalf of AHBA Development and its authorized subcontractors, and are handled under the same protections described here.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and enforce role-based access.
  • To dispatch, track, and validate field work in real time.
  • To record attendance, collections, and expenses accurately.
  • To send operational notifications (assignments, announcements).
  • To keep the Service secure, reliable, and to prevent misuse.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Location Data

Location is used to show authorized dispatchers and administrators the live position and travel history of field teams during work, and to route and verify dispatched jobs. Location is associated with the on-shift work account and is collected only while you are signed in. Where required by the platform, the app requests your permission before accessing location.

4. How Information Is Shared

  • Within your organization: visible to authorized dispatchers and administrators of your own organization for operational purposes.
  • Service providers: we host data on Supabase (database, authentication, file storage) and use Google Play services for app distribution and notifications. These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms.
  • Legal: we may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the Service.

Data is isolated per organization; one subcontractor cannot access another subcontractor's data.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service and to meet operational, legal, and record-keeping requirements. Operational records (jobs, attendance, photos) are kept for the period necessary for validation, billing, and audit, and are then archived or removed. Deleted records are soft-deleted and remain visible only to authorized administrators before final removal.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections, encrypted stored passwords, role-based access controls, and organization-level data isolation. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to legitimate operational and legal retention needs.
  • You can control device permissions (such as location and camera) in your device settings; disabling them may limit app functionality.
  • Because accounts are issued by your employer/organization, some requests may be directed to your organization's administrator.

8. Children

The Service is intended for authorized workers and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, through in-app or other notice.
